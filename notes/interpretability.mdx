Networks are largely black boxes. It is unclear exactly what algorithms they have learned; there's a subfield in machine learning where  
we attempt to "reverse engineer" the code. The best analogy is that the weights of a neural network is a compiled 
computer program; we are attempting to revert to the original, human-readable code that implemented it.
